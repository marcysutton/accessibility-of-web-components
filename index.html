The Typical Experience

Don't forget: there are TWO VIDEOS!

Keyboard Access

The Typical Experience

  • Lack of visual feedback
  • Inability to interact with features
  • Often forgotten as a way to navigate

Screen Reader Access

The Typical Experience

  • No readable text content
  • No image alt attributes
  • Anchors without text
  • Misuse of headings
  • Poor semantics, especially in forms

General Accessibility

The Typical Experience

  • Poor visual contrast
  • No imagination for accessible UX
  • Not for humans
  • No mobile accessibility (That's a thing!)
